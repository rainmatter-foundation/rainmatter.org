Every festive season, organisations gift each other boxes of sweets and dry fruits—and often cheap plastic junk—that are mostly unwanted and end up in the trash. We believe this is unsustainable and wasteful.

We encourage organisations to take the No Corporate Gift Box pledge, and instead, donate the money they would have spent on gift boxes to a worthy cause.
